I Make Neat Visuals for Games, Hopefully Without Breaking Everything

Hi, I'm Jeremy. I create visual effects and technical solutions for games because someone has to make the shiny things sparkle and the complicated things (mostly) work. Whether it's making weather look cool, fixing shader hiccups, or just making sure your game doesn't crash as often, I'm here for it. When I'm not arguing with software or accidentally discovering 'features,' I'm probably thinking up new ideas, experimenting with tools, or looking for an excuse to make something glow.

Professional Background

My journey into game development started at Jyvaskyla University of Applied Sciences, where I worked on projects such as Mortimer: First Launch and Harmony in the Wild. Over the years, I've honed my expertise in creating visually striking effects and solving technical challenges, balancing creativity with problem-solving. Currently, I'm focused on absorbing as much information about Realtime Visual Effects and Shader development as I possibly can, as well as refining and expanding my knowledge of the Unity Engine, tools, and the C# Programming Language.

Skills and Expertise

  • Technical Skills: Unity, VFX Graph, Particle System (Shuriken), Blender, Krita, Photoshop, Illustrator, Embergen, Houdini.
  • Creative Skills: Particle effects, procedural generation, toon-shading techniques, custom render features.
  • Soft Skills: Self-motivation, determination, collaboration, problem-solving, and adaptability.

Personal Interests

When I'm not working on VFX or technical art, you'll probably find me tinkering with PC rigs, exploring interactive art, or diving into the latest games. I also enjoy finding creative ways to bring quirky ideas to life-like turning plumbing pipes into potato launchers for game mechanics! I love iterating on ideas other people pitch at me, finding a way to not only make them work but to add my own personal touch to it (if desired). Outside of my life behind the computer, I'm a father and a husband, I enjoy spending time with my kids and finding new ways to make them laugh. I'm also a big fan of being out in nature, whether it's hiking, fishing, or just finding a quiet spot in the woods to relax - I'm usually at my best when I'm far away from the hustle and the noise of the city.
